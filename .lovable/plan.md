# Proven Design & Consulting, LLC — Website Plan

## What I'll build

A 3-page marketing site for an architectural design + permitting business in Long Beach, CA.

### Pages
1. **Home** — Hero with value prop ("Architectural design and permit-ready blueprints in Long Beach, CA"), services snapshot, why-choose-us, CTA to contact.
2. **Services** — Three service pillars with detail:
   - Architectural Design
   - Permit Set of Blueprints
   - Permitting & City Submittals
3. **Contact** — Contact form (name, email, phone, project type, message), phone/email/address, service-area note (Long Beach + LA County). Form submissions handled via Lovable Cloud (stored in DB, optional email notification).

Shared header/footer with nav, business name, license placeholder, and SEO metadata (per-route titles, descriptions, JSON-LD `LocalBusiness` / `ProfessionalService` schema for local SEO).

### Tech
- TanStack Start (existing template), Tailwind, shadcn components.
- Per-route `head()` for SEO (title, description, og:*, canonical, LocalBusiness JSON-LD with Long Beach geo).
- Lovable Cloud enabled for contact form storage.
